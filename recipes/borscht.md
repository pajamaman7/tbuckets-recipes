# Borscht - Paulette Boyko

A comforting Eastern European beet soup with vegetables, herbs, and a tangy sour cream finish.

## info
* Servings: 3-4
* Prep time: 10 minutes
* Cook time: 15-20 minutes
* Total time: 25-30 minutes

## ingredients

### Vegetables
* 2 beets with tops, slivered
* 1 potato, slivered
* Carrots and vegetables 

### Aromatics & Herbs
* Green onions 
* Parsley 
* Dill 

### Liquids & Finishing
* Water to cover vegetables (about 2" above)
* Chicken broth (quantity to taste)
* Sour cream, thinned with cream
* A bit of flour (for thickening)
* Lemon juice (small splash)
* 1+ teaspoon (ingredient not specified - likely sour cream or butter)

## steps

1. Cook beets, potato, carrots and vegetables about 10 minutes in a pot. Cover with water to about 2" above vegetables.

2. Add green onions, parsley and dill. Simmer 5 minutes.

3. Add chicken broth, 1+ teaspoon, or to taste.

4. Add sour cream thinned with cream, a bit of flour to thicken and some lemon juice. Boil 2-3 minutes. Then serve.

## notes

* **No salt** - Recipe specifically notes not to add salt
* Adjust chicken broth amount to your taste preference
* Flour amount for thickening can be adjusted based on desired consistency
* This scaled-down version makes a small batch, suitable for 1-2 servings
